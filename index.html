Kyle Tycholiz Full Stack Web Developer

I am a full stack web developer with a core strength in React. I take pride in the code that I write, having been a victim of (my own) poorly written code in the past. My strength lies in being able to own the entire front-end of an application, from UI design to state management, and everything in between. Beyond this, I have experience in the back end, and am able to spin up Node servers using Express. Clearly, I favor the ecosystem built around Javascript.

Further to this concept of approaching situations from different angles, in the past several years I have developed a deep interest in other cultures, leading to my hobby of language learning. A main takeaway from this hobby has been this: While most words do have an equivalent in other languages, it is difficult to translate the feeling that each word carries. This means we must think at a deeper level than simply finding the equivalents of each language.
